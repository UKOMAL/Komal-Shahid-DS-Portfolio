## Tips for Personalisation

1. **Research the company's tech stack.** If they use Spark, mention it. If they use Databricks, mention MLflow integration.
2. **Mirror the job description language.** If they say "ML platform" use that phrase, not "MLOps pipeline."
3. **Lead with the metric that matches their domain.** FinTech → AUC/precision. Healthcare → sensitivity/specificity. NLP → F1/BLEU.
4. **Keep it under 400 words.** Hiring managers spend < 90 seconds on cover letters.
5. **End with a specific, low-friction CTA.** Calendly link > "feel free to reach out."
