### 3. Privacy-Preserving Federated Healthcare AI
**Route:** `/projects/federated-healthcare-ai`
**Headline metric:** HIPAA-compliant · Differential Privacy ε ≤ 1.0
**Problem:** Hospitals can't share patient data across institutions, blocking AI training on sufficient data.
**Approach:** Federated learning with differential privacy · homomorphic encryption · GDPR-aligned audit trails
**Tags:** `Federated Learning` `Differential Privacy` `PyTorch` `Healthcare AI` `HIPAA` `GDPR`
**Hover bullets:**
- Zero raw patient records transmitted; model gradients only — validated against HIPAA data minimisation
- Differential privacy (ε = 1.0) with Rényi accountant for rigorous privacy budget tracking
- Cross-silo (hospital-to-hospital) and cross-device (wearable) deployment modes supported
